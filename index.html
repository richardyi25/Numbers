Each turn, a player plays a card from their deck.
The player who played the higher card gains one point.
The card played is then discarded.

Player construct their own decks before starting a game, following 3 rules:

  1. Each deck must contain 15 cards
  2. Each card must have a value between 1 and 5 (inclusive)
  3. The sum of the card values must equal 45

At the end of 15 turns, the player with the most points wins.
In the event of a tie, another game automatically begins.
